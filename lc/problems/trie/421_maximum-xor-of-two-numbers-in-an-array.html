421. Maximum XOR of Two Numbers in an Array

Problem

Given a non-empty array of numbers, $a_0$, $a_1$, $a_2$, … , $a_{n-1}$, where $0$ ≤ $a_i$ < $2^{31}$.

Find the maximum result of $a_i$ XOR $a_j$, where 0 ≤ i, j < n.
